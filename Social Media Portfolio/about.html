Hi there!

My name is Melli and I am a 22 year old Concept Artist and Illustrator from Luxembourg! I am currently in Munich, Germany to pursue studies in Art & Multimedia at the Ludwig - Maximilian - University.

About me

I have been a mainly traditional artist for the longest time, however after discovering Digital Art in 2021, I managed to maintain a passion for both digital and traditional work. I do find joy in pretty much all aspects in art, but I definitely enjoy Character & Creature design the most. Occasionally, I like to create some 3D assets as well, though I am still aiming to improve my skills and become more familiar with Blender. In my personal life, I love playing a wide variety of videogames, reading, taking photos and cooking yummy meals!
